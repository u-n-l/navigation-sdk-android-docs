# Weather

The UNL Maps for Android SDK’s UnlWeatherService class offers a comprehensive suite of weather capabilities. With it, you can retrieve current conditions, hourly forecasts, and daily outlooks - including temperature, air quality, atmospheric pressure, wind speed, UV index, and many more parameters.
